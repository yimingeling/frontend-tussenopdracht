Description

Independently released in 2001 to immediate acclaim by critics and audience alike this body of music proved to be a solid starting point for the then sprouting career of influential producer Yiming Li (known by their stage name of 25FFNEW. Inspired by Madlib/Dilla productions of the late 90's this first full length release would lay the groundwork for the unique sound Yiming would later shape to be.
